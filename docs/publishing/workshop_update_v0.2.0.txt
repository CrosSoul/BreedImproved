Breed Improved v0.2.0

Added player-controlled multi-mode Dynasty cleanup with Bloodline and Negative Congenital Trait modes. Review candidates one by one, spare them, finish early, or add the current and all remaining candidates before final confirmation. The original individual Exile from Dynasty interaction remains available. English and Simplified Chinese interface text has also been improved.
