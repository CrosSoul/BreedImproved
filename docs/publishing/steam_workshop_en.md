# Steam Workshop Copy — English

## Title

Breed Improved

## Subtitle

Exile unwanted members from your Dynasty

## Short description

A player-controlled CK3 utility for exiling one eligible Dynasty member and their descendants into a generated replacement Dynasty.

## Full description

Breed Improved v0.1.0 adds **Exile from Dynasty**, an individual Character Interaction available to a living player-controlled Dynast.

Eligible targets are living, highborn, AI-controlled members of your Dynasty who are neither you, a House Head, nor the Dynast. Minors, adults, unlanded characters, landed rulers, and the current player heir are supported.

After confirmation:

- the target and descendants enter a Dynasty generated by CK3;
- parents and siblings remain unchanged;
- your existing House Head Hook over the target is removed;
- the target receives -60 opinion of you, recovering by 0.5 monthly;
- the target gains 60 base stress;
- the target receives a ten-year modifier with -1 Diplomacy, +20% stress gain, and -0.25 monthly Prestige; and
- the target receives a permanent internal exile marker.

The interaction does not directly change claims, marriages, titles, court status, government, or imprisonment.

## Cost rules

The interaction is free when the target has `bastard`, has `legitimized_bastard`, or has at least one explicitly existing legal parent outside your Dynasty. Otherwise it costs 100 personal Prestige through CK3's native interaction-cost system.

Only script-visible legal parents are checked. Missing parents, spouses, descendants, hidden biological-parent secrets, and ancestry beyond parents do not qualify.

## Compatibility

- CK3: `1.19.*`
- Mod version: `0.1.0`
- No compatibility claim is made for other CK3 versions.
- Compatibility with other mods has not been comprehensively tested. Breed Improved uses prefixed IDs and no `replace_path`, but mods changing the same Dynasty or Character Interaction systems may still interact unexpectedly.

## Supported languages

- English
- Simplified Chinese

## Installation

Steam Workshop is the sole supported installation channel. Subscribe to the Workshop item, enable **Breed Improved** in a CK3 Launcher playset, and launch the game.

## Known behavior and limitations

- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- House Heads, the Dynast, lowborn characters, and player-controlled recipients cannot be targeted.
- Married characters may later receive claims through normal CK3 mechanics; Breed Improved does not directly add or remove claims.
- Bulk Dynasty cleanup is not included in v0.1.0.
- The mod performs no automatic cleanup, background scan, scheduled scan, or recurring action. Every exile requires player initiation and confirmation.
