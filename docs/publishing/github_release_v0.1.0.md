# Breed Improved v0.1.0

The first public release of Breed Improved adds **Exile from Dynasty**, a player-initiated Character Interaction for moving one eligible Dynasty member and their descendants into a replacement Dynasty generated by CK3.

## Changelog

- Added individual Exile from Dynasty interaction.
- Supports minors, adults, unlanded characters, landed rulers, and current player heirs.
- Added conditional free or 100-personal-Prestige native interaction cost.
- Added conditional House Head Hook removal.
- Added recipient opinion, stress, ten-year visible modifier, and permanent exile marker consequences.
- Added English and Simplified Chinese localisation.

## Supported CK3 version and languages

- CK3: `1.19.*`
- Languages: English and Simplified Chinese

Compatibility with other CK3 versions is not claimed.

## Verified runtime behavior

The release-candidate gameplay was verified with minor and adult targets, unlanded and landed targets, the current player heir, descendant propagation, native free/paid cost handling, conditional House Head Hook removal, approved recipient consequences, English and Simplified Chinese localisation, and save/reload persistence.

The Mod performs no automatic background scans or recurring cleanup.

## Known limitations

- House Heads, the Dynast, lowborn characters, and player-controlled recipients cannot be targeted.
- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- Claims, marriages, titles, court status, and government are not directly changed.
- Bulk Dynasty cleanup is not included.
- Compatibility with other mods has not been comprehensively tested.
