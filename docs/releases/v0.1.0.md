# Breed Improved v0.1.0

## Feature Summary

Version 0.1.0 introduces **Exile from Dynasty**, a player-initiated Character Interaction for moving one eligible Dynasty member and their descendants into a replacement Dynasty generated by CK3.

The interaction is available to a living player-controlled Dynast. Eligible recipients are living, highborn, AI-controlled members of the same Dynasty who are not the actor, a House Head, or the Dynast.

## Supported CK3 Version

- Metadata compatibility: CK3 `1.19.*`
- Production acceptance environment: CK3 `1.19.0.6`

## Installation Outline

For local launcher installation from this repository:

1. Keep the `MyCK3Mod/` content directory intact.
2. Copy `MyCK3Mod.mod` to the CK3 user mod directory used by the launcher.
3. In the copied launcher file only, replace `<LOCAL_MOD_PATH>` with the absolute path to the repository's `MyCK3Mod/` directory, using forward slashes.
4. Enable the launcher entry in a CK3 playset.

Do not replace the portable placeholder in the repository copy of `MyCK3Mod.mod`.

## Confirmed Behavior

Production runtime acceptance confirmed:

- correct production interaction visibility;
- correct Simplified Chinese localisation;
- hostile/Dynasty interaction presentation;
- support for minor and adult recipients;
- support for unlanded characters and landed rulers;
- support for the current player heir;
- creation of a generated replacement Dynasty for the target;
- movement of the target's descendants into the replacement Dynasty;
- no change to the Dynasty membership of the target's parents and siblings;
- persistence after save and reload;
- CK3 player-heir recalculation when necessary; and
- no observed CK3 runtime errors or other abnormalities.

Breed Improved does not directly change claims, marriages, titles, court status, government, or other political status during this interaction.

Every exile requires player initiation and confirmation. Version 0.1.0 contains no automatic cleanup, background scan, recurring action, or bulk-processing flow.

## Known Limitations

- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- Bulk Dynasty management is not included.
- House Heads and the Dynast cannot be targeted.
- Player-controlled recipients and lowborn characters cannot be targeted.

## Deferred Future Work

- A married character was observed to gain spouse-related strong claims after game time advanced. This is **DEFERRED — NOT BLOCKING v0.1**. Its cause has not been established, and v0.1.0 does not add claim removal or divorce behavior.
- Bulk Dynasty management remains a separately designed future phase.
- Bastard-trait and biological-parent candidate assistance remains outside v0.1.0.
- Marriage assistance and advanced breeding assistance remain future design work.

No future release date is promised by this document.
