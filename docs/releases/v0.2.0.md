# Breed Improved v0.2.0 — Release Notes Draft

Status: `READY FOR RAY UPLOAD — final runtime acceptance passed; Workshop update not yet published`

## Highlights

Breed Improved v0.2.0 retains the runtime-verified individual **Exile from Dynasty** interaction and adds a player-initiated, multi-mode **Manage Dynasty Cleanup** Decision.

## New in v0.2.0

- Added **Bloodline Cleanup** for public bastard states and the conservative rule requiring two explicitly existing legal parents who are each either lowborn or outside the actor's Dynasty.
- Added **Negative Congenital Trait Cleanup** using the fixed Breed Improved trait preset.
- Added positive congenital-trait warnings during negative-trait review.
- Added sequential candidate review with controls to select, spare, finish early, or add the current and all remaining candidates.
- Added a separate final confirmation before any Dynasty change.
- Added ancestor-first review and selected branch-root folding.
- Added player-managed direct-candidate protection across both modes.
- Reused the existing individual exile outcome for valid selected roots and their descendants.
- Improved English and Simplified Chinese narrative text, option labels, and tooltips.

## Protection boundary

Direct-candidate protection prevents a character from becoming a direct candidate or direct execution root. It is not whole-branch protection. A protected character may still move when they belong to the complete descendant branch of a selected ancestor.

## Preserved v0.1.0 behavior

The individual **Exile from Dynasty** interaction retains its supported target classes, native free-or-100-personal-Prestige cost, conditional House Head Hook removal, opinion and stress consequences, ten-year modifier, permanent exile marker, and descendant movement.

## Player control and exclusions

- No automatic or background scanning.
- No scheduled or recurring cleanup.
- No AI-initiated cleanup.
- No automatic killing, imprisonment, divorce, claim removal, title removal, court movement, or government change.
- Bulk cleanup has no separate Prestige cost in v0.2.0.

## Compatibility and languages

- CK3 compatibility metadata: `1.19.*`
- Languages: English and Simplified Chinese
- No compatibility claim is made for other CK3 versions.
- Compatibility with other mods has not been comprehensively tested.

## Known limitations

- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- Direct-candidate protection does not protect an entire descendant branch.
- Claims, marriages, titles, court status, government, and imprisonment are not directly changed.
- Accepted-founder-parent protection, custom trait selection, scoring, marriage assistance, and advanced breeding assistance are not included.

## Runtime status

The final v0.2.0 artifact passed the complete user-confirmed runtime acceptance matrix. This includes the bilingual layout and quotation revision, all tooltips and removed warning text, every review/confirmation path, candidate and protection rules, complex branch cases, large-Dynasty behavior, save/reload and consecutive-run coverage, and the final CK3 error-log review. Ray recommends release, and Jay/Boss approved the artifact.
