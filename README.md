# Breed Improved

Breed Improved is a player-controlled dynasty management utility for Crusader Kings III.

- Mod version: `0.2.0`
- CK3 compatibility: `1.19.*`
- Languages: English and Simplified Chinese
- Workshop: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3769010534
- Source: https://github.com/CrosSoul/BreedImproved

Version 0.2.0 is runtime-accepted and ready for Ray to upload to the existing Workshop item. Phase 2 passed the complete final acceptance matrix, including both supported languages, all review and confirmation paths, candidate/protection/branch cases, large-Dynasty and save/reload coverage, and the final CK3 error-log review. The Workshop update has not yet been uploaded or published.

## Individual Exile from Dynasty

The v0.1.0 **Exile from Dynasty** Character Interaction remains available. A living player-controlled Dynast can select one eligible member of the same Dynasty and, after confirmation, move that character and their descendants into a replacement Dynasty generated by CK3.

Eligible recipients must be alive, highborn, AI-controlled, in the actor's Dynasty, and neither the actor, a House Head, nor the Dynast. Minors, adults, unlanded characters, landed rulers, and the current player heir are supported when they meet those requirements.

The interaction is free when the recipient has `bastard`, has `legitimized_bastard`, or has at least one explicitly existing, script-visible legal parent outside the actor's Dynasty. Otherwise it costs **100 personal Prestige** through CK3's native interaction-cost system.

After confirmation, the target and descendants enter a generated replacement Dynasty. The interaction removes an existing House Head Hook held by the actor over the target, applies the approved opinion and stress consequences, adds a ten-year visible modifier, and records a permanent exile marker. It does not directly change claims, marriages, titles, court membership, government, or imprisonment.

## v0.2.0 — Manage Dynasty Cleanup

Version 0.2.0 adds the player-initiated **Manage Dynasty Cleanup** Decision for a living player-controlled Dynast.

### Cleanup modes

- **Bloodline Cleanup** finds public bastard cases and characters whose explicitly existing legal father and mother are each either lowborn or outside the actor's Dynasty. One outside parent or a missing parent is not enough.
- **Negative Congenital Trait Cleanup** finds characters with the fixed Breed Improved negative congenital-trait preset. Candidate pages warn when a character also has a positive congenital trait worth preserving.

### Player-controlled review

Candidates are reviewed one at a time. The player may:

- add the current candidate to the exile list;
- spare the current candidate and continue;
- keep the current list and finish review early; or
- add the current and all remaining eligible candidates while preserving earlier decisions to spare someone.

No exile occurs during review. A separate final confirmation is required before any Dynasty membership changes are applied.

### Safeguards and branch handling

- The actor, the actor's ancestors, player-controlled characters, the Dynast, House Heads, dead characters, lowborn characters, characters outside the actor's Dynasty, and individually protected direct candidates are excluded.
- Candidate review is ancestor-first. Selected descendants are folded under a still-valid selected ancestor so the same descendant branch is not deliberately processed as multiple roots.
- Direct-candidate protection prevents a character from appearing as a direct candidate or execution root. It is not whole-branch protection: a protected character may still move when they belong to the complete descendant branch of a selected ancestor.
- Each valid selected branch root and its descendants enter a generated replacement Dynasty through the existing Exile from Dynasty outcome.
- Bulk cleanup has no separate Prestige cost in v0.2.0.

## Player control and automation boundary

Breed Improved performs no automatic cleanup, background scan, scheduled scan, recurring action, or AI-initiated cleanup. Candidate discovery begins only after the player starts and confirms the management Decision. Every exile requires a separate final player confirmation.

## Installation — Steam Workshop

Steam Workshop is the only supported installation channel for ordinary players.

1. Open the [Breed Improved Workshop item](https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3769010534) and subscribe.
2. Enable **Breed Improved** in a CK3 Launcher playset.
3. Launch the game.

GitHub source archives are source snapshots, not supported installable Mod packages. No manual-install ZIP is provided.

## Removal

Disable Breed Improved in the CK3 Launcher and unsubscribe through Steam Workshop. Back up important saves first. Removing the Mod does not roll back Dynasty changes or other consequences already written into a save.

## Compatibility and known behavior

- Compatibility is supported only for CK3 `1.19.*`; other CK3 versions are not claimed.
- English and Simplified Chinese localisation are included.
- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- Claims, marriages, titles, court status, government, and imprisonment are not directly changed.
- Married characters may later receive claims through normal CK3 mechanics; Breed Improved does not directly add or remove those claims.
- Compatibility with other mods has not been comprehensively tested. Breed Improved uses project-prefixed IDs and no `replace_path`, but mods changing the same systems may still interact unexpectedly.
