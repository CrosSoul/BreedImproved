# Breed Improved

Breed Improved is a player-controlled dynasty management utility for Crusader Kings III.

- Mod version: `0.1.0`
- CK3 compatibility: `1.19.*`
- Languages: English and Simplified Chinese

## v0.1.0 — Exile from Dynasty

Version 0.1.0 provides one individual Character Interaction: **Exile from Dynasty**. A living player-controlled Dynast can select one eligible member of the same Dynasty and, after confirmation, move that character and their descendants into a replacement Dynasty generated by CK3.

### Eligibility

The recipient must be:

- alive;
- highborn;
- AI-controlled;
- a member of the actor's Dynasty;
- someone other than the actor;
- neither a House Head nor the Dynast.

Minors, adults, unlanded characters, landed rulers, and the current player heir are supported when they meet those requirements.

### Prestige cost

The interaction is free when the recipient:

- has `bastard`;
- has `legitimized_bastard`; or
- has at least one explicitly existing, script-visible legal parent outside the actor's Dynasty.

Otherwise, the interaction costs **100 personal Prestige** through CK3's native interaction-cost system. Missing parents do not qualify by themselves. Spouses, descendants, hidden biological-parent secrets, and ancestry beyond the recipient's parents are not checked.

### Consequences

After confirmation:

- the recipient and their descendants enter a generated replacement Dynasty;
- the recipient's parents and siblings remain unchanged;
- an existing House Head Hook held by the actor over the recipient is removed;
- the recipient receives `-60` opinion of the actor, recovering by `0.5` each month;
- the recipient gains `60` base stress;
- the recipient receives a visible ten-year modifier with `diplomacy = -1`, `stress_gain_mult = 0.2`, and `monthly_prestige = -0.25`; and
- the recipient receives a permanent internal exile marker.

Breed Improved does not directly change claims, marriages, titles, court membership, government, imprisonment, or other political status. CK3 may recalculate succession and the player heir after Dynasty affiliation changes.

## Player control and excluded features

Breed Improved performs no automatic cleanup, background scan, scheduled scan, or recurring action. Every exile requires explicit player initiation and confirmation.

Version 0.1.0 does not include:

- bulk Dynasty cleanup;
- Decisions or event chains;
- an accepted-founder-parent whitelist;
- ancestry checks beyond legal parents;
- divorce or claim cleanup; or
- background or recurring execution.

## Phase 2 development status — not released

A conservative Phase 2 bulk-cleanup implementation is present in the development source and has passed static review. It is **not part of the released v0.1.0 feature claim**, has not been runtime-verified, and must not be uploaded to Workshop until separately approved.

The development implementation provides one player-initiated Decision with one selected mode per run:

- **Bloodline Cleanup** — public bastard states or both explicitly existing legal parents outside the actor's Dynasty; and
- **Negative Congenital Trait Cleanup** — a fixed, evidence-backed exact-trait preset with positive congenital trait warnings.

Both modes use shared mandatory exclusions, optional individual direct-candidate protection, ancestor-first sequential review, and a separate final confirmation. Direct-candidate protection is not whole-descendant-branch protection. They reuse the unchanged v0.1.0 exile result and add no batch cost. No automatic scan, scheduled action, recurring action, or AI execution is present.

Runtime status remains `NOT RUN`. The required manual acceptance matrix is [docs/testing/phase2_dynasty_cleanup_manual.md](docs/testing/phase2_dynasty_cleanup_manual.md).

## Installation — Steam Workshop

Steam Workshop is the only supported end-user installation channel.

1. Open the **Breed Improved** Workshop item: https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3769010534 and subscribe when it is available to you.
2. Enable **Breed Improved** in a CK3 Launcher playset.
3. Launch the game.

The Workshop version is the official playable release. GitHub source archives are not installable Mod packages and are not supported as an end-user installation method.

## Removal

Disable Breed Improved in the CK3 Launcher and unsubscribe through Steam Workshop if desired. Back up important saves first. Removing the mod does not provide a rollback mechanism for Dynasty changes or other consequences already written into a save.

## Development source

GitHub provides the public source repository, development history, issue reporting, roadmap, documentation, tags, and concise Release notes. Cloning or downloading the repository does not automatically install Breed Improved.

The production CK3 Mod root is `MyCK3Mod/`. Generated Workshop upload content is staged internally under `dist/workshop/BreedImproved/` and is not a public download.

Advanced users may create their own CK3 local Mod entry and point it to the repository's `MyCK3Mod/` production directory. This is a developer-oriented workflow and is not an officially supported end-user installation method.

## Compatibility and known behavior

- Compatibility is supported only for CK3 `1.19.*`; other CK3 versions are not claimed.
- English and Simplified Chinese localisation are included.
- Replacement Dynasty names and coats of arms are generated by CK3.
- Married characters may later receive claims through normal CK3 mechanics. Breed Improved does not directly add or remove those claims, and the cause of the earlier spouse-related claim observation has not been established.
- Lowborn and player-controlled recipients cannot be targeted.
- House Heads and the Dynast cannot be targeted.
- Compatibility with other mods has not been comprehensively tested. Breed Improved uses project-prefixed IDs and does not use `replace_path`.

## Roadmap
